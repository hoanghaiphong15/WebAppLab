Vietnam Destinations & Itineraries

Browse practical itineraries with day-by-day plans, transport tips and local highlights across Northern, Central and Southern Vietnam.

Northern Vietnam (7 days)

Hanoi, Sapa and Ha Long Bay — culture, hill-tribe treks and iconic karst scenery.

  1. Day 1: Arrive Hanoi — Old Quarter walking tour & street food
  2. Day 2: Hanoi museums and train station market
  3. Day 3: Overnight transfer to Sapa — short village trek
  4. Day 4: Full day trekking in Sapa region
  5. Day 5: Return to Hanoi
  6. Day 6: Drive to Ha Long Bay — cruise or day tour
  7. Day 7: Kayaking or cave visit & depart

Central Vietnam (4–6 days)

Da Nang, Hoi An and Hue — beaches, UNESCO sites and vibrant food scenes.

  • Day 1: Arrive Da Nang — Marble Mountains & beach time
  • Day 2: Transfer to Hoi An — evening lantern walk
  • Day 3: Cooking class or My Son Sanctuary visit
  • Day 4: Travel to Hue — imperial citadel and river sampan

Southern Vietnam (3–5 days)

Ho Chi Minh City and the Mekong Delta — markets, temples and river life.

  • Day 1: Explore Ho Chi Minh City — war history sites and Ben Thanh Market
  • Day 2: Day trip to Cu Chi Tunnels or city neighborhoods
  • Day 3: Mekong Delta — floating markets and village visits
